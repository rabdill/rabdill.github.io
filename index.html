About

My name is Rich. I love working in DevOps, I ride a motorcycle, I hang out with a hedgehog named Chalupa. My favorite color is green, but it used to be blue. My favorite book is Catch-22, and my favorite sport is spreadsheets.

What I do

I'm currently a systems engineer at Gannett Co., where my focus is on supporting the infrastructure for Gannett sites including USA Today, Tripology, and The Point. My work is almost exclusively within Amazon Web Services, where I assist in the architecture and maintenance of systems encompassing EC2, RDS, Route 53, SQS, S3, CloudWatch... you get the idea. Much of my work (and my interest) lies in automation and continuous integration, and I'm very familiar with systems such as Jenkins and Chef.

It's within the DevOps/systems automation arena that I find the most enjoyment – I love writing code, especially when the result is a big, red button that will do my work for me.
